Spreadr enables WooCommerce merchants to find and sell products from Amazon. To learn more about Spreadr, visit https://spreadr.co/woocommerce

== Description ==
Spreadr plugin helps merchants import Amazon products to WooCommerce stores and earn affiliate commissions or run your dropshipping business.

= Easily import products =
Find products from Amazon and import them to your WooCommerce store in just a few clicks. Expand with hundreds of new products within minutes. Amazon AWS keys not required to get started.

= Hassle-free new income =
Earn money when someone buys a product. Amazon manages shipping, returns, inventory and customer support. Effortless new revenue for you.

= Increase your influence =
Curate products that complement your brand or grow completely new categories to appeal to wider audiences. Become bigger and more valuable.

== How it Works ==
Fast and simple
1. Import any product from Amazon to your Woocomemrce store in 1-click. AWS keys not required to get started.
2. Your visitors can view and buy the products. Orders get fulfilled by Amazon.
3. Amazon pays you commission for each sale. Signup for Amazon Affiliate Program - its free. You can track orders and commissions in the dashboard.

= Expand your store with products you never had = 
Choose from over a million products available on Amazon to add to your product catalog. Add products which complement your store and align with your brand. Make it bigger and more valuable.

= Curate products and showcase your taste =
Curate tasteful products to give your audience a new reason to come back.

= More products – more pages – more traffic =
You can modify title and description of the products imported to your store. More pages with optimized descriptions lead to better SEO and increase in organic traffic.

Hassle-free new revenue stream =
While Amazon manages inventory, customer support and shipping, you get up to 10% as commission from all qualifying purchases.

= Dropshipping business with Amazon =
Alternative to affiliate income, you can use Spreadr to make Amazon as the drop-shipping source. Instead of linking out to Amazon, you can collect orders on your Woocommerce store and later fulfill them yourself.

= Reach new audiences =
Add new product categories that complement your brand. Appeal to wider audience and monetize your influence.

== Features ==
Simple yet Powerful

1. Fast & Easy Product Import – Just provide the Amazon product link & click Add. We use official Amazon API to pull product info.
2. Consistent Store UI – Amazon products will be displayed in same look and feel of your store. ‘Add to cart’ button will be replaced with ‘View on Amazon’ button.
3. Customize product name, description, and other details after importing the products.
4. Keep 100% of Your Commissions.
5. Auto-update - App auto updates price & availability values of imported products to match Amazon so that your inventory and prices are up to date.
6. Uninstall proof - Even if you remove the plugin, imported products continue to link to Amazon helping you earn commissions.
7. Bulk-import - Provide a list of Amazon products and get them imported all at once.
8. Many more features - Auto-tags, Geo-localize, Markup Pricing, Currency conversion etc.
